# 🚀 Projects

## 1️⃣ Toy Store Sales Dashboard

### Overview

An interactive sales performance dashboard built to analyze revenue, profitability, order trends, and product performance for a retail toy business.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor business performance and identify high-performing products and categories.

---

### Business Questions Answered

- What is the total revenue generated?
- How much profit has the business earned?
- How many orders have been placed?
- How do sales perform over time?
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which product categories contribute the most revenue?

---

### Key KPIs

✅ Total Revenue

✅ Total Profit

✅ Total Orders

---

### Dashboard Features

- Revenue tracking
- Profit analysis
- Monthly order trend analysis
- Product-level performance evaluation
- Category-level revenue analysis
- Interactive filtering and exploration

---

### Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

### Business Value

This dashboard enables business users to:

- Monitor overall business performance
- Identify profitable products
- Track sales trends
- Support inventory and sales decisions
- Improve revenue optimization strategies
